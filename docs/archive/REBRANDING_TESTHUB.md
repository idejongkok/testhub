## Brand Story

### What is TestHub?

TestHub is a modern quality assurance platform designed to be the central hub for all your testing needs. Whether you're managing test cases, planning test runs, tracking bugs, or analyzing automation results, TestHub provides a unified, intuitive interface to streamline your QA workflow.

### Why "TestHub"?

The name "TestHub" conveys:
- **Test**: Clear focus on quality assurance and testing
- **Hub**: Central platform that brings everything together
- **Simple & Memorable**: Easy to say, spell, and remember
- **Professional**: Sounds established and trustworthy
- **Scalable**: Name works well as features expand

### Target Audience

- QA Engineers & Testers
- QA Managers & Leads
- Development Teams
